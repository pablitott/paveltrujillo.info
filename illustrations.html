THE PIT AND THE PENDULUM

This project was about a chilling compilation of some of Edgar Allen Poe's best-loved stories. I had the opportunity to capture my own vision of the story "The Pit and the Pendulum" while trying to experiment with traditional illustration and later adding some digital texture.
