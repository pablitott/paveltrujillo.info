RUDECATS

This project was created for a company who wanted something bold and American as their new image for a T-Shirt line. According to their brief, the company wanted to show their main target how their designs can be worn by a group of cool, bold, and fun-loving people.

PROBIOTA

This company is based in Oaxaca, Mexico. This company's goal is to create natural fertilizer that is planet friendly. The logo represents the idea that the fertilizer is friendly with Mother Nature, and that it takes care of the land by providing proper nutrients. It helps customers reap quality products grown from the land.

OKI

This optometry business needed a friendly eye-catching logo. The company wanted to show its clients that "kindness" does matter. They differentiate the services from those of other similar companies by emphasizing the kindness of their service, multiple options they offer, as well as the typical vision test.

UNIDAD DE CONGRESOS

According to the brief, the client, Instituto Mexicano del Seguro Social, needed a logo for one of the buildings they were leasing. Very important conferences, business meetings, artistic performances and Congressional meetings take place there. Therefore, they needed a clean, minimalist, and formal image to properly represent their building.

MISSISSIPPI SOULS

This is a band of blues main based in Mexico City, who play at bars there nightly during the week. The client's brief requested the band's vintage style of the old blues genre that they play.

The band not only plays at bars but at other different types of stage still representing their old blues.
